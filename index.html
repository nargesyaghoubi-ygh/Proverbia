Featured Proverbs

Every cloud has a silver lining

هر ابری یک پوشش نقره ای دارد

Even in the darkest or most difficult times, there is always some hope or positive aspect to be found. Challenges often bring unexpected benefits or lead to personal growth.

개천에서 용 난다

"A dragon rises from a small stream."

This Korean proverb means that even someone from humble beginnings can rise to greatness. It celebrates perseverance, talent, and unexpected success.

When there's a will, there's a way

وقتی اراده هست راهی هست

This proverb means that with strong willpower and determination, no obstacle is too great. Persistence and inner drive can lead to success even in the face of difficulties.

Proverbs Of The Day

Actions speak louder than words

This proverb means that what people actually do carries more weight and truth than what they merely say. Actions reflect true intentions and character, while words can be empty or deceptive. It emphasizes the value of behavior over promises.
